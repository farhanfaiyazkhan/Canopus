Two burning flames.

Two burning flames
In a cold windy night
Complementing each other
It is such an alluring sight,
Riding together
Tumbling around
Kindling their lover
Illuminating the surround,
Sensing wind stirring up
They wrapped abound
Contemplating a large plop
Of rain falling down
They breathed their love
Before they drown.

A never ending voyage.

A never ending voyage,
Beholding the beauty of vast sea
I lost all my rage
The blue sky at horizon is turning pink
The source of life is returning
Returning back to his life
Contemplating this, my heart almost sank.

I sat mesmerized
Watching the past becoming present
I was hypnotized, lost in moment
I set the sail
Destined to fail, maybe
The breeze of love is sweeping us forward
I felt the caress of her breath
Perhaps, the almighty is writing
Two never ending love stories.

Life is a race.

Life is a race,
A race without a starting line
A race so indefinite,
A race with your own self
An unbiased game with biased help,
Run or you’ll fall behind
And always keep this in your mind
Don’t get too far away
Otherwise, you’ll lead yourself astray.
